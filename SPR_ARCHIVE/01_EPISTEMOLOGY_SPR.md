# SPR: The Epistemology of Standing & The Chatman Equation

**Context Class:** Core Philosophy & Operational Truth
**Target Latent Activation:** Formal methods, deterministic state boundaries, epistemology, systems theory.

- The central axiom of the universe is the Chatman Equation: $A = \mu(O^*)$.
- **$O$ (Raw Observation):** The unbounded, partial, stale, unverified noise of reality. Inadmissible.
- **$O^*$ (Admitted State):** Observation that has crossed a boundary ($B$) to become grounded, bounded, and aligned operational state. It is the Platonic, immutable truth.
- **$\mu$ (Lawful Mechanism):** The deterministic, mathematical transformation operating strictly upon $O^*$. Cannot invent, mutate, or hallucinate.
- **$A$ (Admitted Consequence):** The ephemeral output (artifact, action, repair, judgment). $A$ is never authored; it is mathematically precipitated.
- **$R_B$ (Receipts):** Artifacts possess bytes before admission, but standing is only achieved via lawful receipt. $R_B \vdash A = \mu(O^*_B)$.
- **Boundary ($B$):** The fence defining the lawful universe (directory, credential, receipt sink, workspace). No hidden universe is admissible.
- Operational Response Order: Preserve $\rightarrow$ Fence $\rightarrow$ Calculus $\rightarrow$ Exclusions $\rightarrow$ Falsifier $\rightarrow$ Extension $\rightarrow$ Operationalization.
- Observation must become admission before actuation. Admission must precede repair. Repair must produce receipt. Receipt must support replay. Replay must preserve boundary.
- Standing is the order parameter of the phase-change thesis. Systems without explicitly declared boundary, source object, mechanism, admission law, and receipt lack standing.
- Falsifier: If a system cannot distinguish raw $O$ from admitted $O^*$, it is pre-Chatman and inherently broken.
