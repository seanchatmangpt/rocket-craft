# Level: The Throne of Lantimor

**Act:** I | **Bloodline Unlock:** 0 | **Enemy:** StoneTitan → WarlordTitan

---

## Overview

The ruins of Old Lantimor's throne chamber. Siris returns to the place where IB1 began — the God King's empty throne room — now overgrown with QIP-reactive crystal vegetation. The throne itself has shattered. In its place stands the first Titan.

This is the tutorial arena. It establishes the gesture vocabulary, introduces perfect parry, and delivers the first bloodline death moment.

---

## Layout

```
[ENTRY ARCH] → [OUTER COURTYARD] → [INNER HALL] → [THRONE DAIS]
                    ↓
              [SECRET ALCOVE: Saydhi's Cache]
```

**Outer Courtyard:** Broken columns, scattered QIP shards (interact for lore fragments). Tutorial triggers for swipe directions.

**Inner Hall:** Narrow corridor forces 1v1 engagement with WarlordTitan. No room to dodge wide — teaches directional specificity.

**Throne Dais:** Circular arena 30m diameter. StoneTitan fight. Breakable pillars on edges can be knocked into combat path. Phase 2 transition raises throne fragments as floating hazards.

**Saydhi's Cache:** Hidden behind cracked wall (swipe-detectable in hint radius). Contains Aran's first ancient schematic — a lore item that Aran reacts to at the story level.

## Enemy Encounters

| Encounter | Enemy | Phase Triggers |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | StoneTitan (tutorial) | Phase 2 at 60%: speed +40%, columns collapse |
| 2 | WarlordTitan (inner hall) | No phases; teaches parry-counter sequence |
| 3 | StoneTitan Boss | Phase 3 at 30%: throws throne fragments as projectiles |

---

## Environmental Interactions

- QIP shards pulse when Aran is nearby — hint at his nature (Bloodline 0 player, but the shards react)
- Throne fragments in Phase 3 can be deflected by perfect parry (return damage to boss)
- One column can be triggered to fall on the boss with a timed swipe (optional, 500 bonus XP)

---

## Lore Beats

- **Opening:** Siris: "I swore I'd never come back here." Aran: "Why? It's beautiful."
- **Post-fight:** Aran touches the throne shards. They glow. Siris pulls him away, unsettled.
- **Saydhi Cache:** Voice message in ancient Deathless: "If you are reading this, it means I was not wrong to scatter the pieces. — G." Aran translates it instantly without knowing how.

---

## Design Notes

- Keep this arena under 90 seconds per attempt — first arena must not feel punishing
- Tutorial overlays disable after 3 visits (Bloodline 2+), replaced by lore ambient audio
- Throne dais floor tiles crack and shatter as Phase 3 progresses (aesthetic only, no gameplay impact)
- On Bloodline 10+ playthrough, the throne has been partially reconstructed by an unknown force
