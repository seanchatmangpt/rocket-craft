# Freedom Gundam (ZGMF-X10A)

**Series:** Mobile Suit Gundam SEED (Cosmic Era)
**Gacha Tier:** SSR

## Lore
Stolen from ZAFT's top-secret facility by Kira Yamato with the help of Lacus Clyne, the Freedom Gundam represented the pinnacle of CE mobile suit technology at its time of creation. Powered by a Neutron Jammer Canceler and nuclear fusion reactor, Freedom boasts nearly unlimited operational time. Its METEOR support unit and Full Burst Mode — simultaneously firing all weapons at once — made Kira nearly invincible in battle, earning him the title "The Ultimate Coordinator."

## Unique Mechanic: Full Burst Mode & SEED Factor
- SEED Factor: Passive — when HP drops below 40%, SEED activates for 5 turns (ATK +20, SPD +15, all parries become perfect parries)
- Full Burst Mode: Spend 80% SEED gauge → simultaneously fire all weapons (beam sabers, railguns, cannons) for 8× hit salvo
  - Each hit deals (ATK × 0.6) damage; total output 4.8× ATK; ignores Phase Shift reduction
  - 8-weapon lock: Cannot use individual weapons during Full Burst activation (2-turn commitment)
- Phase Shift Armor: Passive — first 2 physical hits per battle deal 0 damage; depletes after that (can recharge by spending 40% SPC gauge)
- METEOR Link (Ultimate): If "METEOR" Striker Pack equipped, Full Burst adds 6 additional missile salvos

## Signature Move: Full Burst
- Gesture: Swipe Right → Swipe Left → Swipe Up → Hold 1.0s → Release
- Effect: All 8 weapons fire simultaneously; 8-hit salvo; total 4.8× ATK damage; can interrupt any enemy attack animation
- Cooldown: 200 seconds
- Visual: Freedom spreads wings fully; blue beam lattice fills entire screen

## Combo Chain
Standard IB4 chain (1.0×/1.5×/2.0×/3.0×). During SEED Factor, chain extends to IB5 (bonus hit at 3.5×) with free Full Burst cancels at depth 4+.

## Parry Type
Standard: 220ms broad / 70ms perfect (SEED Factor upgrade: perfect window extends to 90ms during SEED active)
