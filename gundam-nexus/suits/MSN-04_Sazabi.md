# Sazabi (MSN-04)

**Series:** Mobile Suit Gundam: Char's Counterattack (Universal Century)
**Gacha Tier:** SSR

## Lore
Char Aznable's final mobile suit, the Sazabi was purpose-built as his answer to Amuro's Nu Gundam — a red behemoth of nearly double the mass, with a Psycoframe cockpit tuned to amplify Char's own Newtype resonance. Where Amuro built the Nu for precision and last-resort defense, Char built the Sazabi for absolute, overwhelming offensive dominance. The suit's six remote Psycho Fin Funnels are larger than most mobile suit weapons; its beam shot rifle fires rounds the size of tanks. In the asteroid field above Earth, it was the most powerful suit ever fielded by Zeon.

## Unique Mechanic: Red Comet Speed & Psyco Fin Funnels
- Red Comet Passive: Every 5th consecutive attack deals 3× damage (Char's legacy — he always moves three times faster)
  - Streak counter resets only if an attack misses or player takes damage
  - Visual indicator: Sazabi leaves red aftertrails at streak ×3
- Psyco Fin Funnels: Larger than Nu Gundam Fin Funnels; 3 active at once (vs. Nu's 6 smaller units)
  - Each Psyco Funnel deals 2× Nu Funnel damage per hit
  - Newtype gauge at 40%+: 1 Psyco Funnel auto-defends (blocks 1 attack per 6 turns)
  - Newtype gauge at 70%+: All 3 funnels deploy simultaneously — triple strike (3× 2× ATK damage)
- Mega I-Field: DEF +20 for 3 turns; blocks ALL ranged attacks during window; costs 80% Newtype gauge
- HP Pool: Sazabi has 150 base HP (vs. standard 100) — reflecting its massive physical frame

## Signature Move: Psyco Funnel Barrage
- Gesture: Swipe Left → Swipe Right → Swipe Left → Hold 1.2s → Release
- Effect: All 3 Psyco Funnels converge with beam shot rifle simultaneously; 3× [ATK+SPC]/2 + 1× ATK rifle bonus; total 4× effective damage; enemy knocked back (position reset)
- Cooldown: 160 seconds
- Visual: Three massive red beam lances converge with rifle blast; shockwave visible

## Combo Chain
