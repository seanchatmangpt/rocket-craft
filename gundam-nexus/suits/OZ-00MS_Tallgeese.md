# Tallgeese (OZ-00MS)

**Series:** Mobile Suit Gundam Wing (After Colony)
**Gacha Tier:** SR

## Lore
The great-grandfather of every OZ mobile suit and the spiritual ancestor of the five Gundams themselves, Tallgeese was the prototype from which all After Colony mobile suit design descended. It was shelved as "too powerful for mass production" — the stress it placed on human pilots was considered lethal to anyone below the operational ceiling. Zechs Merquise, who recovered and restored it, described the experience of piloting Tallgeese as "fighting the suit itself." Its Dober Gun railcannon remains one of the most powerful single-shot weapons in the AC roster.

## Unique Mechanic: G-Force Threshold & Prototype Superiority
- G-Force System: Passive — the higher Tallgeese's SPD becomes, the more pilot stress accumulates
  - SPD buffs above +20 from base trigger G-Force gauge fill (+5 per turn at max speed)
  - G-Force at 50%: ATK +10, SPD +5 (pilot pushing through the pain)
  - G-Force at 100%: Pilot Overload — ATK +25, SPD +15 for 3 turns; then SPD -30 and DEF -20 for 5 turns (Zechs blackout penalty)
  - G-Force mechanic is unique to Tallgeese; no other suit has it
- Prototype Data: Passive — against any OZ-derived mobile suit (enemy faction OZ units in story mode), Tallgeese deals +20% damage (counter-analysis of own descendants)
- Dober Gun: Semi-charged ranged weapon; Swipe Down + Hold 1.0s → fires at full caliber
  - Full caliber: 2.5× ATK; ignores 30% DEF; knockback guaranteed
  - Quick draw (no charge): 1.5× ATK; instant; no knockback
- Vernier Dash: SPD burst ability — triple SPD for 1 turn (cooldown: 10 turns); creates shockwave on arrival (0.5× ATK AoE, small radius)

## Signature Move: Dober Gun Maximum Caliber
- Gesture: Hold 2.5s → Swipe Down → Swipe Down → Release
- Effect: Dober Gun fires at absolute maximum charge; 4.0× ATK damage; ignores all DEF; penetrates barriers (I-Field, Phase Shift, GN Field); G-Force gauge immediately fills to 80%
- Cooldown: 200 seconds
- Visual: Classic Tallgeese shoulder pose; Dober Gun glows orange; projectile visible as physical railgun slug
