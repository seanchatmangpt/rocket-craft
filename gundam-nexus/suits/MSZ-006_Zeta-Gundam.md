# Zeta Gundam (MSZ-006)

**Series:** Mobile Suit Zeta Gundam (Universal Century)
**Gacha Tier:** SSR

## Lore
Developed by Anaheim Electronics under Project Zeta, the MSZ-006 Zeta Gundam introduced the transformable mobile suit concept that defined an era of UC design philosophy. Piloted primarily by Kamille Bidan — a Newtype of extraordinary power — the Zeta's bio-sensor system amplifies the pilot's emotions into combat output, turning rage, grief, and love into raw power. Kamille's sacrifice at Zeta's conclusion, his mind shattered by Paptimus Scirocco's psychic assault, remains one of UC's most enduring tragedies.

## Unique Mechanic: Wave Rider Transform
- Passive: Zeta has two modes — Mobile Suit (MS) and Wave Rider (WR)
- Transform: Double-tap dodge button during any movement → transforms for 3 turns
- Wave Rider Mode:
  - SPD +30, ATK +18; all dash attacks become "charge" type (pierce DEF by 20%)
  - Dodge input becomes a high-speed ram (deals 40 damage, no cooldown)
  - Cannot use shield or standard parry in WR mode (defensive input redirected to charge attack)
  - Auto-reverts to MS mode after 3 turns; 15-turn cooldown before next transform
- Bio-Sensor Surge: When HP drops below 30%, SPC +25 for 4 turns (Kamille's emotional peak state)
- Hyper Mega Launcher: MS mode only; 2-turn charge; deals 1.8× ATK; +20% if Bio-Sensor active

## Signature Move: Wave Rider Crash
- Gesture: Transform → Swipe Left → Swipe Left → Swipe Up (must be in Wave Rider mode)
- Effect: Full-speed aerial ram; 2.5× (ATK+SPD)/2 damage; ensured knockdown; enemy stunned 1 turn
- Cooldown: 120 seconds (Wave Rider mode must be active to trigger)
- Visual: Zeta unfolds wings mid-transform, crashes through enemy in streak of blue light

## Combo Chain
Dual-mode chain. MS mode: standard IB4 (1.0×/1.5×/2.0×/3.0×). WR mode: aggressive 3-hit chain (1.5×/2.5×/4.0×) — no depth-1 opener; every WR hit enters at 1.5× multiplier minimum.
