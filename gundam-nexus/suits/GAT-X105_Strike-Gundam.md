# Strike Gundam (GAT-X105)

**Series:** Mobile Suit Gundam SEED (Cosmic Era)
**Gacha Tier:** SR

## Lore
The first of Earth Alliance's five G-Project Gundams to be deployed in combat, the Strike Gundam was stolen — or rather, defended — by Kira Yamato when ZAFT attacked Heliopolis. The Strike's core innovation is its Striker Pack system, which allows rapid mid-combat reconfiguration between Aile (flight), Sword (melee), and Launcher (artillery) loadouts. More than any other suit, Strike represents adaptability as a philosophy; it is not the most powerful Gundam, but in the hands of the right pilot, it can counter almost anything.

## Unique Mechanic: Striker Pack System
- Three base Striker Packs included at unlock (no additional purchase):
  - Aile Striker: SPD +25; enables flight mode; ATK +8 (beam rifle)
  - Sword Striker: ATK +20 (Schwert Gewehr); DEF +5 (anti-beam shield); aerial melee chains
  - Launcher Striker: SPC +15; ranged AoE (Agni cannon); 2-turn charge for maximum output
- Rapid Swap: Swap Striker Pack mid-combat in 0.5 seconds (Swipe Up+Down); no cooldown penalty
- SEED Potential: When HP drops below 25%, SEED activates (all stats +15 for 3 turns; Strike's Coordinator limit lifts temporarily)
- Phase Shift Absorb: Each physical hit blocked by PS Armor restores 3 SPC gauge (energy recovery from impact)

## Signature Move: Agni Cannon Full Power (Launcher Mode)
- Gesture: Equip Launcher Striker → Hold 2.0s → Swipe Down → Release
- Effect: Agni fires at maximum charge; 2.8× ATK damage; AoE (hits all enemies in cone); Phase Shift does not reduce this hit
- Cooldown: 180 seconds (Launcher Striker must be equipped)
- Visual: Orange beam column from Agni barrel; explosion radius fills half-screen

## Combo Chain
Pack-dependent chain. Aile: IB4 air chain (1.0×/1.5×/2.0×/3.0×); Sword: IB3 heavy (1.0×/2.2×/4.0×); Launcher: IB2 burst (1.5×/5.0× — only 2 hits but massive multiplier). Swapping packs mid-combo resets chain depth.

## Parry Type
Standard: 220ms broad / 70ms perfect (Phase Shift Absorb triggers on every broad parry; no bonus on perfect)

## Striker Pack Compatibility
Aile / Sword / Launcher (base kit — all three included). Also compatible with Ootori and I.W.S.P. packs (tournament unlock).
