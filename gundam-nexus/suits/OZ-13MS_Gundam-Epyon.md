# Gundam Epyon (OZ-13MS)

**Series:** Mobile Suit Gundam Wing (After Colony)
**Gacha Tier:** SSR

## Lore
Built by Treize Khushrenada as his personal instrument of war and given to Heero Yuy — who then traded it to Zechs Merquise — the Epyon carries a dark legacy. The suit has no ranged weapons whatsoever, an intentional philosophical statement by Treize: war should be fought face-to-face, in heat and proximity, not at a distance. Its ZERO System is identical to Wing Zero's, but calibrated for a different temperament. In Zechs's hands, the Epyon became perhaps the most feared melee combatant in the AC era.

## Unique Mechanic: ZERO Berserker & No Ranged Mode
- No Ranged Weapons: Epyon has zero ranged attacks. All damage is melee or heat rod (semi-ranged whip).
  - Heat Rod: Extending whip weapon — reaches "medium range" but counts as melee; inflicts "Burn" debuff (3 damage per turn for 3 turns)
  - This restriction is permanent; no Striker Pack grants ranged capability to Epyon
- ZERO Berserker Mode: Unlike Wing Zero's ZERO which the pilot can resist, Epyon's ZERO has lower sanity threshold
  - Triggers at 3 perfect parries (vs. Wing Zero's 5) — faster, less controllable
  - Berserker Active (4 turns): AI takes FULL control — player input disabled; suit executes optimal melee combo automatically; cannot be stopped
  - ensured kill on non-boss enemies during Berserker; ensured 150 damage vs. bosses (cannot be reduced by DEF or RES)
  - After Berserker: Returns to player control; RES -30 for 5 turns (ZERO hangover)
- Mobile Armor Form: Double-tap dodge → mobile armor dragon form for 2 turns (SPD +40, immune to ground attacks; DEF +20; cannot attack normally — only ramming)
- Heat Rod Mastery: Burn stacks accumulate; at 3 stacks, Burn becomes "Inferno" (8 damage per turn; 4 turns; enemy -10 DEF while burning)

## Signature Move: Epyon Heat Whip Full Extension
- Gesture: Swipe Right (full screen width) → Hold 1.0s → Release (melee range only)
- Effect: Heat rod extends to maximum range; wraps around enemy; 3.0× ATK + Inferno burn ensured; enemy immobilized for 2 turns (cannot dodge or parry); if ZERO Berserker is active, deals 4.5× ATK instead
- Cooldown: 145 seconds
- Visual: Red heat rod glows white-hot; wraps enemy in electrical burn; Zechs's visor glows red

## Combo Chain
Melee-exclusive IB5 chain (1.0×/1.8×/2.6×/3.8×/5.5×). Highest per-step multiplier in roster for a 5-step chain. Heat Rod substitutes for ranged at depth 3+ (auto-fires between melee hits). ZERO Berserker resets chain to depth 1 and begins auto-executing (AI takes over; player cannot input).

## Parry Type
ZERO-Assisted: 190ms broad / 55ms perfect (ZERO predicts enemy melee; perfect parry auto-triggers Heat Rod riposte at no cost — Burn debuff applied immediately)
