# Gundam 00 Raiser (GN-0000+GNR-010)

**Series:** Mobile Suit Gundam 00 (Anno Domini)
**Gacha Tier:** SSR

## Lore
The marriage of the 0 Gundam's twin GN Drives in a single frame, Gundam 00 represented a conceptual breakthrough: instead of a support unit providing one drive, the 00 Raiser combined both Setsuna's 00 Gundam with Saji Crossroads's 0 Raiser support craft, creating a resonance between two GN particle fields that unlocked Trans-Am Burst — the ability to saturate the surrounding area with GN particles, awakening dormant Innovade potential and enabling quantum communication with any living consciousness in range. In battle, its performance was beyond anything the military-industrial complex could plan for.

## Unique Mechanic: Twin Drive System & Trans-Am
- Twin GN Drives: Passive — GN particle gauge fills 2× faster than single-drive suits
  - GN Field: When gauge at 40%+, deploy GN field barrier (blocks 1 ranged attack per 4 turns; free activation)
  - Resonance Burst: At 100% gauge, both drives synchronize — SPC +15 for 3 turns; all attacks leave GN particle trail (debuffs enemy RES -10 per hit, stacks to -30)
- Trans-Am: Spend 100% GN gauge → activate Trans-Am for 5 turns (vs. standard 3-turn duration for other suits)
  - Trans-Am effects: ATK ×1.5, DEF ×1.5, SPD ×3 (only suit with SPD ×3 multiplier; others get ×2)
  - After Trans-Am: 10-turn cooldown with ATK -20, DEF -20, SPD -20 (particle burnout)
- Trans-Am Burst (Ultimate): Requires 0 Raiser docked (combined form) → Trans-Am Burst activates
  - AoE GN particle saturation: All enemies in range have RES halved for 5 turns; allies gain +15 to all stats
  - Burst duration: 3 turns; cannot be cancelled once started
- Quantum Brainwaves: While Trans-Am is active, perfect parries trigger "Quantum Read" — display next 3 enemy attack types before they occur

## Signature Move: Trans-Am Raiser Sword
- Gesture: Trans-Am active + 0 Raiser docked → Swipe Left → Swipe Right → Hold 1.0s → Release
- Effect: 00 Raiser forms Raiser Sword (GN particle blade the size of a warship); 4× ATK + 2× SPC damage; guaranteed crit; slices through all active enemy barriers; enemy DEF halved for 4 turns
- Cooldown: 200 seconds (Trans-Am must be active; 0 Raiser must be docked)
- Visual: Twin GN Drives glow simultaneously; massive particle blade extends from emitter; vertical slash

## Combo Chain
Accelerated IB4 chain (1.0×/1.5×/2.0×/3.0×). At combo depth 4+, Trans-Am auto-triggers if GN gauge is at 60%+ (faster threshold than any other suit; others need 100% gauge for Trans-Am). During Trans-Am, IB4 upgrades to IB5 (adds 4.5× final hit).
