# Gundam Aerial (XVX-016)

**Series:** Mobile Suit Gundam: The Witch from Mercury (Ad Stella)
**Gacha Tier:** SSR

## Lore
Built by Elnora Samelin (Prospera Mercury) as a vessel for her daughter Ericht's consciousness, Gundam Aerial is not merely a mobile suit — it is a living weapon, inhabited by a GUND-format AI that is simultaneously the suit's operating system and a person named Eri. Suletta Mercury, believing Aerial to be a gift from her mother, develops a profound bond with the suit — never fully understanding that her closest companion lives inside the machine itself. Aerial speaks, responds, and fights not as a tool, but as a partner.

## Unique Mechanic: GUND-Format AI (Eri System) & Permet Score
- Eri Speaks: Passive — Aerial communicates with the player through UI prompts
  - At combat start, Eri provides one hint about the enemy's dominant attack pattern
  - At HP 50%, Eri alerts player to next 3 enemy attacks (shown as timing indicators)
  - Hints cannot be turned off (lore-accurate; Eri always speaks)
- Permet Score Gauge: Grows through combat depth (unique accumulation mechanic)
  - Score 1 (0% gauge): Base stats
  - Score 2 (25%): Shell units unlock — remote bits extend ATK range by 30%
  - Score 3 (50%): Permet barrier active — blocks one attack per 5 turns (Eri's choice, not player's)
  - Score 4 (75%): Eri co-pilots — SPD +20, auto-dodges one attack per 4 turns
  - Score 5 (100%): Full GUND sync — all attacks deal +40% damage; Eri takes over combo finishers (AI-controlled, ensured perfect execution)
  - Score 6 (beyond 100%): Emergency threshold — Suletta and Eri merge temporally; 8-turn window, all stats +50, then Permet burnout (ATK/DEF/SPD halved for remainder of match)
- Shell Unit Formation: At Score 2+, shell bits form different patterns based on player gesture
  - Swipe Right: Line formation (piercing shot, 2× ATK)
  - Swipe Up: Cross formation (AoE burst, 1.5× ATK all targets)
  - Swipe Circle: Cage formation (traps enemy for 1 turn, no damage)

## Signature Move: Permet Flower
- Gesture: Score 4+ → Swipe Circle → Hold 1.5s → Release
- Effect: All shell units arrange in bloom pattern; simultaneous beam convergence; 5× SPC damage; inflicts "Permet Resonance" debuff (enemy SPC and RES -25 for 3 turns)
- Cooldown: 190 seconds
- Visual: Pink-gold beam petals bloom from shell formation; Eri's voice says "Together, Suletta."
