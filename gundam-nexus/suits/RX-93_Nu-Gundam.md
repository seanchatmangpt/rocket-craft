# Nu Gundam (RX-93)

**Series:** Mobile Suit Gundam: Char's Counterattack (Universal Century)
**Gacha Tier:** SSR

## Lore
Amuro Ray's final mobile suit, the Nu Gundam was designed by Amuro himself and represents the culmination of Federation mobile suit development. Its Psycoframe cockpit allows direct neural interface between Newtype pilot and machine, enabling the legendary I-Field Barrier and Fin Funnel coordination that stopped Axis at the cost of almost everything. In the climactic battle over Earth, Amuro's Newtype power amplified through the Psycoframe to move an asteroid — an act that transcended the limits of man and machine alike.

## Unique Mechanic: Fin Funnels
- Passive: At Newtype gauge 50%+, 1 Fin Funnel auto-parries per 8 turns (free, no HP cost)
- At 80%+: 4-Funnel formation activates — after any perfect parry, 4 auto-targeting ranged shots (5 damage each)
- I-Field: Spend 100% Newtype gauge → deploy barrier blocking next 3 attacks (10-second window)
- Funnel Cage: Signature combo — requires Newtype gauge 60%+; all funnels deploy simultaneously; traps enemy for 2 turns (cannot attack or dodge)

## Signature Move: Funnel Storm
- Gesture: Swipe Up → Swipe Right → Hold 1.5s → Release
- Effect: All 6 Fin Funnels fire in convergence; 6× [ATK+SPC]/2 damage; ignores 30% DEF
- Cooldown: 180 seconds
- Visual: Gold beam trails converge on enemy

## Combo Chain
Standard IB4 chain (1.0×/1.5×/2.0×/3.0×). At depth 4+, Fin Funnels auto-target during each attack animation (+5 bonus damage per attack).
