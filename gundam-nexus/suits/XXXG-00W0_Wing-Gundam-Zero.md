# Wing Gundam Zero (XXXG-00W0)

**Series:** Mobile Suit Gundam Wing (After Colony)
**Gacha Tier:** SSR

## Lore
The original Gundam, predating all five Wing Gundams, Wing Zero was designed by the five scientists who would later create each pilot's suit. Its ZERO System directly interfaces with the pilot's neural pathways, flooding them with battlefield data and future-state projections — at the cost of sanity. Only pilots with exceptional willpower can resist the system's seductive pull toward total destruction. Wing Zero Custom, its refined EW form, remains the most visually iconic Gundam in Western markets.

## Unique Mechanic: ZERO System
- Passive: Each perfect parry charges ZERO System by 20%
- At 100% (5 perfect parries): ZERO System activates for 4 turns
  - During ZERO active: ATK +25, SPD +20; all attacks gain "foresight" tag (cannot miss)
  - Side effect: Lose 8 RES per turn (sanity drain); if RES hits 0, ZERO Berserker triggers (suit acts uncontrolled for 2 turns)
- ZERO Foresight: Once per activation, predict enemy's next attack and pre-empt with a guaranteed counter (deals 1.5× normal damage)
- Twin Rifle Pre-charge: While ZERO is inactive, holding charge attacks for 2+ seconds stores energy in both cannons for a buffed next shot (+30% ATK)

## Signature Move: Twin Buster Rifle Full Burst
- Gesture: Hold 2.0s → Swipe Down → Swipe Down → Release
- Effect: Combine both rifle halves; deal 3× ATK damage in a wide cone; 50% chance to one-shot non-boss enemies; boss enemies take -40% DEF for 3 turns
- Cooldown: 240 seconds (reduced to 150s while ZERO System is active)
- Visual: Massive white energy beam consumes screen; angel wings spread fully

## Combo Chain
Modified IB5 chain (1.0×/1.4×/1.8×/2.5×/4.0×). At depth 5, ZERO System auto-activates if gauge is at 80%+. Angel Wing Slash replaces the final combo hit (aerial dive, unblockable).
