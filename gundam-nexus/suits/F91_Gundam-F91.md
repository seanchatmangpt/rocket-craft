# Gundam F91 (F91)

**Series:** Mobile Suit Gundam F91 (Universal Century)
**Gacha Tier:** SSR

## Lore
Designed by the Strategic Naval Research Institute decades after the One Year War, the F91 was the first Federation Gundam small enough to match Zeon's mobile suit scale — a deliberate philosophical return to efficiency over brute force. Piloted by Seabook Arno during the Crossbone Vanguard's invasion of the Frontier colonies, the F91's Variable Speed Beam Rifle and Bio-Computer system made it devastatingly precise in close-quarters combat. The VSBR's ability to adjust beam compression speed mid-fire made it equally effective against hardened armor and agile targets alike.

## Unique Mechanic: VSBR & Bio-Computer
- Variable Speed Beam Rifle: Toggle between two fire modes with swipe gesture
  - Rapid Mode: 3 fast shots (ATK × 0.5 each); fires in 0.8 seconds; good against agile targets
  - Penetration Mode: 1 charged shot (ATK × 2.0); 1.5s charge; ignores 50% DEF; good against armored targets
  - Toggle: Swipe Up during any idle state (no cooldown; instant)
- Bio-Computer Overload: At SPD threshold (SPD stat × active turns bonus reaches 9,000 combined), F91 generates afterimage clones
  - Afterimages: 2 false targets appear; enemy AI alternates targeting between clones and real suit; reduces incoming hit rate by 40%
  - Duration: 4 turns; clones disappear when F91 takes a hit
- Speed Burst: Once per 20 turns, SPD doubles for 2 turns (F91 becomes unhittable by standard tracking attacks)
- VSBR Energy: Each Rapid Mode shot charges VSBR gauge; at 6 stacks, Penetration Mode deals +50% bonus damage

## Signature Move: VSBR Maximum Output
- Gesture: Hold Penetration Mode charge 3.0s → Swipe Up → Release
- Effect: VSBR fires at maximum compression; 3× ATK damage; ignores all DEF; creates afterimage burst (auto-clones for 2 turns)
- Cooldown: 160 seconds
- Visual: F91's visor glows gold; VSBR barrel superheats; beam punches clean through target
