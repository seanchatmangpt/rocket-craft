# Unicorn Gundam (RX-0)

**Series:** Mobile Suit Gundam Unicorn (Universal Century)
**Gacha Tier:** SSR

## Lore
Designed by Cardeas Vist as the key to the enigmatic "Laplace's Box," the RX-0 Unicorn Gundam appears in its default state as a non-threatening white mobile suit with its horn sealed — a deliberate aesthetic choice to evoke innocence and peace. But when threatened, the NT-D System activates, unlocking the Destroy Mode: the suit fractures along its Psycoframe skeleton, glowing with rose-gold light, and its performance increases dramatically. Piloted by Banagher Links — who initially resisted becoming a soldier — the Unicorn became the symbol of Newtypes' final hope for coexistence with Oldtypes.

## Unique Mechanic: NT-D System (Destroy Mode)
- Two Modes: Unicorn Mode (default) and Destroy Mode (NT-D active)
- Unicorn Mode: Standard stats as listed; no special passive; conserves NT-D gauge
- NT-D Activation: Tap both thumbs simultaneously → Destroy Mode activates (12-turn duration)
  - Destroy Mode: ATK +20, SPD +25, DEF -5; suit fractures and glows rose-gold
  - NT-D Steal: While in Destroy Mode, if enemy uses a funnel or remote weapon, NT-D can hijack it (once per activation; stolen funnel becomes a one-time ally attack for 3× ATK damage)
  - Psycoframe Resonance: At 80%+ NT gauge in Destroy Mode, all attacks gain "NT Resonance" tag (ignore 25% DEF)
- Awakening Pulse: Once per match, when HP reaches 0 in Destroy Mode, Psycoframe resonates — recover to 40% HP and gain 50% NT gauge (Banagher's will)
- Phenex Echo: If Phenex variant is in party, NT-D activates 2 turns earlier and ATK bonus increases to +28

## Signature Move: NT-D Full Burst Saber
- Gesture: Destroy Mode → Swipe Up → Swipe Up → Hold 2.0s → Release
- Effect: Beam magnum fires simultaneously with beam saber extended swing; 3.5× ATK + 1× SPC damage; NT Resonance ensured on this hit; stolen funnel (if active) fires simultaneously
- Cooldown: 170 seconds (Destroy Mode must be active)
- Visual: Rose-gold Psycoframe flares; suit silhouette fractures in light; beam magnum detonates

## Combo Chain
Mode-split chain. Unicorn Mode: conservative IB4 (1.0×/1.4×/1.8×/2.5×). Destroy Mode: aggressive IB5 (1.2×/1.8×/2.5×/3.5×/5.0×). Transitioning to Destroy Mode mid-chain preserves current depth and adds 1 bonus depth step.

## Parry Type
NT-Enhanced: 200ms broad / 65ms perfect. Destroy Mode: 185ms broad / 50ms perfect (NT-D elevates reaction speed; NT Steal triggers on perfect parry if enemy funnel is active)
