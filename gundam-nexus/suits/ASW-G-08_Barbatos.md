# Gundam Barbatos (ASW-G-08 — Base Form)

**Series:** Mobile Suit Gundam: Iron-Blooded Orphans (Post Disaster)
**Gacha Tier:** SR

## Lore
Excavated from beneath the surface of Mars by the child soldiers of Chryse Guard Security — who had been using it as a power generator — the original Gundam Barbatos was over 300 years old, its organic components still viable because the Gundam Frame chassis was designed to endure. Mikazuki Augus established direct Alaya-Vijnana connection with Barbatos through spinal implant surgery performed with a screwdriver; there was no medical facility and no time. What Barbatos lacked in modern weapons systems, it compensated for with raw, brutal efficiency. It fought like something that remembered how to survive.

## Unique Mechanic: AVJ Synchronization (Learning)
- AVJ Foundation: Barbatos's AVJ is the base implementation before full spinal integration
  - Cost: 3% HP per turn while AVJ is active (vs. Lupus Rex's free activation)
  - During AVJ: ATK +12, SPD +10; melee attacks gain nanolaminate pierce
  - AVJ Learning: Each battle increases Barbatos's synchronization depth permanently
    - After 5 battles: HP cost reduced to 2% per turn
    - After 15 battles: HP cost reduced to 1% per turn
    - After 30 battles: HP cost eliminated (Barbatos fully synchronized — effectively becomes a second Lupus Rex unlock at stat-level)
- Nanolaminate Armor: Passive — physical attacks reduced by 10% flat; beam attacks reduced by 15% flat
- Scrappy Fighter: When any equipment is "broken" (enemy destroys a Striker Pack or weapon), Barbatos gains ATK +8 permanently for that match ("fighting with what you have" bonus)
- 300-Year Legacy: Passive — Barbatos never loses morale. All stat debuffs applied to Barbatos expire 1 turn early.

## Signature Move: Earth Drop Mace
- Gesture: Hold 1.5s → Swipe Down → Swipe Down → Release (works at any HP level)
- Effect: Overhead drop using full suit mass and gravity; 2.8× ATK; guaranteed knockdown; if AVJ is active, adds 1× ATK secondary slam (Mikazuki feels it through the spine)
- Cooldown: 120 seconds
- Visual: Barbatos jumps, uses thrusters to crash down; impact crater; rust-and-blood aesthetic

## Combo Chain
Primal IB3 chain (1.0×/2.0×/4.0×). Only 3 steps but highest jump per step in roster (step 2 doubles; step 3 doubles again). Encourages short, devastating chains over depth-5 combos. During AVJ, chain gains a free 4th hit at 5.0× (if HP cost is paid that turn).

## Parry Type
AVJ-Enhanced: 215ms broad / 78ms perfect (costs 2% HP on perfect parry but grants auto-counter at 1.5× ATK; broad parry is free but grants no counter)

## Striker Pack Compatibility
Mace Upgrade Pack — increases mace weight and head; ATK +10; Earth Drop damage increased to 3.5× ATK
