# Justice Gundam / Infinite Justice Gundam (ZGMF-X09A / ZGMF-X19A)

**Series:** Mobile Suit Gundam SEED / SEED Destiny (Cosmic Era)
**Gacha Tier:** SR

## Lore
Athrun Zala's counterpart to Kira's Freedom, the Justice Gundam was built alongside Freedom in ZAFT's top-secret facility and stolen at the same time. Where Freedom specialized in ranged dominance, Justice was built for close combat and interception — its Fatum-00 backpack functioned as both a sub-flight lifter and an independent attack drone. In SEED Destiny, Athrun piloted the redesigned Infinite Justice, which refined every aspect of the original while adding a Fatum-01 with enhanced close-range capability. Both suits reflect Athrun's philosophy: a Coordinator who fights not for superiority, but for the people he loves.

## Unique Mechanic: Fatum System & SEED Sync
- Fatum-01 Independent Operation: Fatum backpack can detach and fight independently
  - Detach: Swipe Down — Fatum separates and auto-attacks for 3 turns (deals 0.7× ATK per turn, autonomous)
  - Reattach: Tap anywhere while Fatum is deployed — grants speed boost on return (SPD +10 for 2 turns)
  - Fatum Barrier: While attached, Fatum's beam shield extends Justice's DEF +10
  - Fatum Cannon: While attached, Swipe Left activates Fatum's beam cannon (1.5× ATK, medium range)
- SEED Sync (Cooperative): If Freedom/Mighty Strike Freedom is also in active party
  - "Kira & Athrun Bond": Both suits' SEED activation threshold lowers to 50% HP (from standard 40%)
  - Combo Finish: After any depth-4+ chain, auto-trigger Fatum follow-up for free (1× ATK bonus)
- Lacerta Beam Sabers (×2): Dual-wield active — depth-3 chain hits twice simultaneously (no extra input)
- Variable Phase Shift: Spend 30 SPC gauge → temporarily boost Phase Shift to stop beam damage for 2 turns

## Signature Move: Fatum Full Attack
- Gesture: Fatum attached → Swipe Up → Swipe Left → Swipe Down → Release
- Effect: Justice leaps to enemy while Fatum launches independently; simultaneous dual-beam attack from two vectors; 2.2× ATK (suit) + 2.0× ATK (Fatum) = 4.2× ATK total; cannot be parried from both angles
- Cooldown: 150 seconds (Fatum must be attached at initiation)
- Visual: Athrun and Fatum perform synchronized aerial cross-slash; two green beam trails cross in X pattern

## Combo Chain
Dual-saber IB4 chain (1.0×/1.6×/2.2×/3.5×). At depth 3, Lacerta dual-wield triggers automatically (depth 3 hits twice; effective 4.4× multiplier at depth 3 instead of listed 2.2×). Fatum follow-up available after any chain as a free bonus if detached.

## Parry Type
Standard: 220ms broad / 70ms perfect (Fatum proximity warning extends broad window to 235ms when Fatum is deployed nearby)

## Striker Pack Compatibility
Fatum-01 (built-in, not a separate pack — this is Justice's native backpack). No additional Striker slot unless Fatum is detached (then slot opens for 2-turn window; any pack can be inserted temporarily).
