# Turn A Gundam (WD-M01)

**Series:** Turn A Gundam (Correct Century)
**Gacha Tier:** SSR

## Lore
Buried beneath the Sea of Tranquility for millennia, the Turn A Gundam is the Black History's most powerful weapon — the suit that ended not one civilization but many, across countless cycles of humanity's rise and fall. Piloted by Loran Cehack, a Moonrace who chose Earth as his home, the Turn A's Moonlight Butterfly nanomachine system can dissolve all technology on a planetary scale. Syd Mead's distinctive design, with its mustache motif and rounded forms, was controversial at launch — but the Turn A's place as the most powerful Gundam in canon is now unquestioned.

## Unique Mechanic: Moonlight Butterfly & Universal Compatibility
- Universal Adapter: Turn A can equip ANY Striker Pack in the game (only suit with this property)
  - Striker effects remain fully active even when switching mid-combat (no 3-turn lockout)
  - Each Striker Pack equipped grants +5 to one base stat determined by pack type
- Moonlight Butterfly: Ultimate ability — once per match only
  - Gesture: Hold 3.0s (anywhere on screen) → Draw a circle
  - Effect: Unblockable AoE; deals 150 flat damage to all enemies on screen; cannot be reduced, parried, or dodged
  - Cooldown: Entire match (single use); fills only at match start
- Black History Archive: Passive — for every 10 turns survived, RES +3 (stacks up to +30)
- Adaptive Repair: Once per combat, when HP drops to 0, Turn A automatically recovers 25 HP (Black History self-repair nanomachines)

## Signature Move: Moonlight Butterfly
- Gesture: Hold 3.0s → Draw circle gesture (AR touch)
- Effect: Nanomachine wings unfurl; 150 unblockable flat damage to all targets; no defense reduction applies; stuns all non-boss enemies 3 turns
- Cooldown: Single use per match (fills at match start, cannot refill)
- Visual: White nanomachine wings spread to horizon; slow-motion dissolve of enemy armor

## Combo Chain
Universal IB5 chain (1.0×/1.3×/1.7×/2.2×/3.5×). Chain works identically regardless of equipped Striker Pack. No depth penalty for switching Striker mid-chain (unique among all suits).

## Parry Type
Standard: 225ms broad / 75ms perfect (Black History Archive adds +5ms perfect window per 10 survival turns, up to +30ms)

## Striker Pack Compatibility
ALL PACKS — Turn A is the only suit with universal Striker compatibility. No restrictions.
