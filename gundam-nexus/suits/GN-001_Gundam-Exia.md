# Gundam Exia (GN-001)

**Series:** Mobile Suit Gundam 00 (Anno Domini)
**Gacha Tier:** SSR

## Lore
Celestial Being's close-combat specialist, the GN-001 Exia was designed around a single philosophy: a mobile suit that could defeat any opponent in close quarters using seven distinct blade weapons simultaneously. Piloted by Setsuna F. Seiei — a former child soldier for KPSA who found purpose in Celestial Being's mission to end war — Exia's Seven Swords system made it simultaneously graceful and terrifying. In its damaged R2 configuration after four years of hunted isolation, Exia achieved a Trans-Am resonance that bordered on the miraculous.

## Unique Mechanic: Seven Swords System
- Seven Swords: Exia has 7 blade weapons; each has a unique gesture trigger
  1. GN Sword (main): Tap — 1.2× ATK slash
  2. GN Long Blade: Swipe Down — 1.5× ATK extended reach
  3. GN Short Blade: Swipe Up — 0.8× ATK fast hit; cannot be parried
  4. GN Beam Daggers (×2): Swipe Left or Right — 1.0× ATK each; can be thrown (ranged)
  5. GN Beam Sabers (×2): Hold 0.5s — 1.8× ATK heavy; only activates at ATK combo depth 3+
- Seven Swords Rotation: Cycling through all 7 weapons in one combat session (using each at least once) triggers Seven Swords Mastery — ATK +15 permanently for that match
- GN Drive Overdrive: Spend 80% SPC gauge → ATK +20, SPD +20 for 3 turns; GN particle trail on all attacks (enemy RES -5 per hit)
- Trans-Am (Solo): Single-drive Trans-Am lasts 3 turns (standard); ATK ×1.5, SPD ×2.5; after burnout: stats -15 for 8 turns (harsher penalty than 00 Raiser's twin-drive recovery)
- Damaged R2 Mode: At HP below 35%, Exia enters R2 aesthetic state (visual only, but SPC +10 — "Setsuna's will" bonus)

## Signature Move: Seven Swords Full Deploy
- Gesture: Use all 7 swords at least once → Swipe Up → Swipe Down → Hold 1.5s → Release
- Effect: All 7 weapons deploy simultaneously in a whirlwind assault; 7 rapid hits at ATK × 0.8 each = 5.6× ATK total; final hit from GN Sword cannon mode deals 2× ATK bonus; ignores enemy parry
- Cooldown: 175 seconds (Seven Swords Mastery must be active)
- Visual: Exia moves so fast it appears as multiple afterimages; 7 distinct blade trails visible

## Combo Chain
Multi-weapon IB5 chain (1.0×/1.5×/2.0×/3.0×/4.5×). Each chain step automatically uses the next sword in Seven Swords rotation (auto-cycles). At depth 5, Seven Swords Mastery activates if not already triggered (+ATK bonus applies retroactively to this chain).

## Parry Type
Standard: 215ms broad / 65ms perfect (Speed-optimized; at Seven Swords Mastery active, perfect window extends to 55ms and grants auto-counter with GN Beam Dagger throw)
