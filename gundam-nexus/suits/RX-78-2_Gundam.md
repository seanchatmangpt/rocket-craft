# RX-78-2 Gundam

**Series:** Mobile Suit Gundam (Universal Century 0079)
**Gacha Tier:** SR

## Lore
The original. The suit that launched everything. The RX-78-2 was developed in secret at Side 7 by the Earth Federation's V Project, and was never supposed to be piloted by a teenager — but Amuro Ray was there when Zeon attacked, and the war's course changed forever. While technically surpassed by virtually every suit in this roster, the RX-78-2 remains the spiritual foundation of all Gundam design philosophy: one suit, one pilot, against impossible odds. Its combat data formed the basis for every Federation mobile suit that followed.

## Unique Mechanic: Legacy Core & Unlimited Upgrade Potential
- Legacy Core: RX-78-2 has no stat cap on upgrades — all enhancement items apply at 150% efficiency (other suits cap at 100%)
  - The Original's potential: Each upgrade tier grants +2 bonus stat points (others grant +1)
  - Maximum upgraded RX-78-2 can theoretically reach any SSR suit's stat ceiling
- Historical Lore Unlock: Surviving 30 turns in any battle triggers "One Year War Archive" cutscene + permanent +5 to all stats (one-time per suit)
  - Additional Archives unlock at 60 turns, 100 turns (each adds +5 to one stat of player's choice)
- Core Fighter Escape: Once per match, eject core fighter — Amuro escapes to 30% HP recovery before returning to cockpit (2-turn retreat; cannot be attacked during retreat)
- V-Project Data: At match start, RX-78-2 scans enemy and displays base DEF and HP values (no other suit provides this intelligence)
- Gundam Hammer: Unique weapon — Swipe Circle gesture activates hyper-dense ball-and-chain; 2.5× ATK, always crits

## Signature Move: Last Shooting (Beam Rifle Point-Blank)
- Gesture: Hold 1.5s → Swipe Down → Swipe Up → Release
- Effect: Run directly at enemy; fire beam rifle at zero range; 2.8× ATK damage; guaranteed stagger (enemy cannot act next turn); if enemy HP below 30%, additional beam saber follow-up (1.5× ATK bonus)
- Cooldown: 140 seconds
- Visual: Classic RX-78-2 charge scene; visor flares yellow; beam rifle glows

## Combo Chain
Starter IB4 chain (1.0×/1.4×/1.8×/2.8×). Lowest multipliers in roster but each hit charges Legacy Core upgrade gauge (+1 stack per hit). Every 50 stacks, one base stat permanently increases by 1 (uncapped; unique mechanic).

## Parry Type
Standard: 230ms broad / 80ms perfect (widest broad window in roster — reflects Amuro's foundational precision training)
