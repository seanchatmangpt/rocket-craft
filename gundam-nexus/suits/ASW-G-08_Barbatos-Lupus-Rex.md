# Gundam Barbatos Lupus Rex (ASW-G-08)

**Series:** Mobile Suit Gundam: Iron-Blooded Orphans (Post Disaster)
**Gacha Tier:** SSR

## Lore
The third and final evolution of the Gundam Frame unit excavated by Tekkadan, Barbatos Lupus Rex was reborn after the original Lupus took catastrophic damage during the Mars conflict. Mikazuki Augus, whose Alaya-Vijnana system was implanted directly into his spinal column — sacrificing the full movement of his right arm and leg — achieved perfect synchronization with the frame that no other pilot could replicate. The Rex form abancompletedd ranged weapons entirely, embracing Barbatos's nature as a ground-combat berserker with a mace the size of a battleship corridor.

## Unique Mechanic: Alaya-Vijnana Junction (AVJ)
- AVJ Built-In: Barbatos Lupus Rex has AVJ fully integrated (no HP cost to activate — unique in roster)
  - Other suits pay 5% HP per AVJ activation; BLR activates free at any time
- AVJ Active: ATK +20, SPD +15; all melee attacks gain "nanolaminate pierce" tag (ignores beam-resistant DEF)
- Mace Mastery: All mace attacks deal 1.3× base multiplier (applies before combo multiplier)
  - At AVJ depth 3+ (sustained 3+ turns): Mace gains "shockwave" tag — hits all enemies in a 5-meter radius
- Berserker Threshold: When HP drops below 20%, BLR enters Berserker state automatically
  - Berserker: AVJ permanently active (no timer); ATK +35 total; cannot use any non-attack input (no parry, no dodge); auto-attacks every turn
  - Berserker cannot be cancelled; ends only when enemy is defeated or HP reaches 0
- Nanolaminate Shield: Passive — beam attacks reduced by 20% flat (stacks additively with DEF reduction)

## Signature Move: Celestial Mace Strike
- Gesture: AVJ active → Hold 2.0s → Swipe Down (any direction) → Release
- Effect: Overhead mace slam; 3.5× ATK damage; ensured ground-bounce (enemy airborne 2 turns — cannot attack); shockwave hits all adjacent enemies for 1.5× ATK
- Cooldown: 130 seconds (AVJ must be active)
- Visual: Red AVJ eye illuminates; mace glows with heat friction; crater forms on impact

## Combo Chain
Melee-only IB4 chain (1.3×/2.0×/3.0×/5.0× — all multiplied by 1.3× Mace Mastery base). No ranged attacks in chain. At depth 4, shockwave auto-activates on final hit (no input needed). Chain resets on dodge instead of breaking.

## Parry Type
AVJ-Enhanced: 210ms broad / 80ms perfect (AVJ allows body-reading that widens broad window; no SPC gauge cost)
