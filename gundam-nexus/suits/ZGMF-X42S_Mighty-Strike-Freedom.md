# Mighty Strike Freedom Gundam (ZGMF-X42S)

**Series:** Mobile Suit Gundam SEED Freedom (Cosmic Era)
**Gacha Tier:** SSR

## Lore
The culmination of every CE mobile suit design philosophy, Mighty Strike Freedom was built for Kira Yamato by COMPASS as the ultimate expression of his belief that a Gundam should protect, not destroy. Capable of transforming between Mobile Suit, mobile armor, and a hybrid "Mighty" configuration that merges both forms, the suit's SEED Destiny System synchronizes with Kira's elevated SEED consciousness — granting combat awareness that borders on the prophetic. In the skies above the Founder's colony, its performance bordered on the mythological.

## Unique Mechanic: Mighty Form & SEED Destiny
- Three Forms: Mobile Suit (MS) → Strike Form (SF, high-speed) → Mighty Form (MF, combined assault)
  - Swipe Down+Left simultaneously to cycle forms (3-turn cooldown per transition)
- Mighty Form: Merges MS and mobile armor configs; ATK +20, SPD +10, DEF +15; all attacks gain "Mighty" tag
  - Mighty-tagged attacks pierce Phase Shift; cannot be reduced below 50% damage by any DEF buff
- SEED Destiny System: Passive — accumulates gauge through depth-4+ combos; at 100%, activates Transcendence Mode
  - Transcendence: 6-turn duration; all attacks multi-hit (×2 hits each); ATK +30; SPD +25
  - Transcendence cannot be dispelled; ends naturally after 6 turns
- Full Burst Mighty: In Mighty Form, Full Burst fires all weapons in both MS and armor configs (12-weapon salvo instead of 8)

## Signature Move: Mighty Full Burst
- Gesture: Enter Mighty Form → Hold 2.5s → Swipe Right → Swipe Right → Release
- Effect: 12-weapon salvo; 6× ATK per hit; 2 hits ensured; targets cannot block during animation (1.5s lockout); total 12× ATK potential
- Cooldown: 210 seconds (Mighty Form must be active)
- Visual: Wings unfold to maximum span; golden SEED aura detonates across battlefield

## Combo Chain
Triple-mode chain: MS IB4 standard → SF IB3 speed (1.2×/2.0×/3.5×) → MF IB4 power (1.5×/2.0×/3.0×/5.0×). Form transitions mid-combo do not reset depth counter.
